# E-Commerce Customer & Sales Analysis (SQL)

## Objective
Analyze e-commerce sales data to understand customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends.

## Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)
- MS Excel

## Dataset
- Generated 8,000+ synthetic records using SQL
- Includes order details such as product, category, customer ID, quantity, and price

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Total Revenue
Calculated overall revenue using quantity and price

### 2. Monthly Revenue Trend
Analyzed month-wise revenue performance

### 3. Top-Selling Products
Identified products with highest sales and revenue

### 4. Repeat Customers
Detected customers placing multiple orders

### 5. Customer Retention
Identified loyal customers with more than 3 purchases

## Key Insights
- Certain products contribute significantly to total revenue  
- Repeat customers form an important segment of overall sales  
- Monthly trends show variation in revenue performance
